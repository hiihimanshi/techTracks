Mechanical

Mechanical engineering is a diverse field that focuses on the design, development,manufacturing, and maintenance of mechanical systems and devices. engineers work in various industries, including automotive, aerospace, energy, manufacturing, and robotics, where they design everything from engines and turbines to HVAC systems and consumer products

Architecture

Architecture is both an art and a science, focusing on the design, planning, and construction of buildings and structures that are aesthetically pleasing, functional, and sustainable. Architects are responsible for creating spaces that meet the needs of their users while considering factors such as safety, accessibility, and environmental impact.

Civil

Civil engineers are responsible for designing and overseeing the construction of roads, bridges, dams, buildings, airports, water supply systems, and other structures that form the foundation of our communities engineers utilize principles of mathematics, physics, and materials science to plan, design, and execute projects that meet safety, environmental, and economic requirements

Aerospace

It is a specialized field of engineering that focuses on the design, development, testing, and production of aircraft, spacecraft, satellites, and other aerospace vehicles and systems. Aerospace engineers apply principles of aerodynamics, propulsion, materials science, and structural analysis to create vehicles that can operate within Earth's atmosphere or in the vacuum of space.
